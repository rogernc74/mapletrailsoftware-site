How it works

Capture now. Reflect later. Grow the memory over time.

You do not need to write a perfect journal entry in the moment. You only need enough to save it now so it is still there when you have a quiet minute tonight.

1. Capture a moment instantly

Write a quick note, save a photo, or speak from Apple Watch before the moment slips away.

2. Turn small moments into a real journal

When the day slows down, your saved moments give you something real to build from instead of a blank page.

3. Add the details around the day

Context like weather, people, music, health, and place helps you remember not just what happened, but what the day felt like.

4. Revisit the days that mattered

The timeline makes it easy to move back through the moments you want to keep instead of losing them in the pace of life.

5. Let the app carry more of the memory

Integrated context means activities, workouts, weather, and other day signals can help the memory feel fuller without extra work from you.

6. Watch your gallery grow

As you keep capturing, the Memory Gallery unlocks milestones so the app reflects progress, streaks, and the shape of your lived life.

Why it matters

Built from a real regret.

Moments Later started with a wish — that there had been a way to capture more of what happened when my kids were small. The little things they said. The ordinary afternoons. Now they are grown and on their own, and some of those moments are just gone.

This app is the one that would have helped then. It is here now for anyone still in those years — or anyone who simply wants to hold on to what is happening today.

— Roger, Founder

About MapleTrail

Thoughtful software, built with warmth.
