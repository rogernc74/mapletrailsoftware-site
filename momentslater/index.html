How it works

Capture now. Reflect later. Remember better.

The idea is simple: make it incredibly easy to save a moment in the instant it happens, then give you better ways to revisit and enrich it when you have time.

1. Save the spark

Log a quick sentence, voice note, prompt, place, or simple tag the second something meaningful happens.

2. Add depth later

When you return, AI can help expand a rough note into a fuller memory, reflection, or journal-style entry.

3. Revisit your life

Browse a timeline of your moments so the small parts of life do not disappear into the blur of busy days.
