Privacy Policy for MapleTrail Software and Moments Later

This Privacy Policy describes how MapleTrail Software handles information related to its website and apps, including Moments Later.

Effective date: April 4, 2026

Information you provide

MapleTrail Software may receive information you choose to provide directly, such as when you contact support or send feedback by email.

App content

Moments Later is designed as a personal product for saving moments, memories, and reflections. Information you create in the app is used to provide the app experience.

Moments, journal entries, attached media, and related app content are stored privately for your use. If you use iCloud sync, that content syncs through your personal private iCloud/CloudKit account across your Apple devices.

Permissions and device access

Moments Later requests access only when you choose to use a feature that depends on it.

This can include camera access for capturing photos or video, microphone and speech recognition access for voice capture and transcription, location access for adding place context, notification access for reminders, and optional access to Calendar, Apple Health, Apple Music, and Journaling Suggestions for richer journal context.

If you connect Strava, Moments Later uses read-only access to retrieve activity context for the day you are journaling. It does not post to Strava or use Strava as a social feed.

Analytics

Moments Later can send anonymous feature-usage analytics only if you enable that option in Settings. These analytics are limited to product usage information, such as opened screens and capture types, and do not include your moment text, transcripts, photos, videos, locations, people, or tags.

Website data

The MapleTrail Software website does not use a public account system. Basic technical information may be collected by hosting providers or standard server logs to operate, secure, and improve the site.

Third-party services

Depending on the features you choose to use, Moments Later may rely on Apple services such as iCloud/CloudKit, Calendar, Apple Health, Apple Music, notifications, or speech recognition. If you enable anonymous analytics, analytics events are processed by PostHog. If you choose to connect Strava, Strava data is used only for the journal-context feature you request.

Sharing

MapleTrail Software does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, or protect the rights, safety, and integrity of users and MapleTrail Software.

Data retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide support, operate the app or website, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Your choices

You can contact MapleTrail Software to ask privacy-related questions or request help with your information.

You can also manage app permissions through your device settings, turn notifications on or off, disconnect optional integrations, and disable anonymous analytics at any time.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@mapletrailsoftware.com.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
